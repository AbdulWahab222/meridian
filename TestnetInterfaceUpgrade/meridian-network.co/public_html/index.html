How can you ensure the liquidity will remain in the Uniswap pool ?

After launching the Meridian Vault, we will remove the liquidity from Uniswap, swap the remaining MRDN for LOCK, and re-add both ETH and LOCK at the ratio we initially removed them. After doing so, we will send the corresponding UNI-V2 tokens to the burn address, thereby ensuring we won’t be able to remove liquidity ever again.

Who is the team ?

Both co-founders are seasoned business owners and consultants in various industries (e-commerce, SaaS, etc). The core team consists of Cryptopinions as lead solidity developer, Ragnar as UI designer, two part-time front-end developers, and Che, our recently hired community manager.

Why is the team anonymous ?

Initially the team decided not to disclose their identity as this was something not everyone was comfortable with. We did not see it as a priority. We have come to realize it is actually a topic of concern for some of our community members, and decided to re-evaluate our decision sometime between our Vault launch and our DAO balancer pool launch.

What is Meridian Network

Born as an alternative to current DeFi protocols, Meridian Network is set to become the first ecosystem of dapps/protocols to be community run, governed by its own users through a set of DAO-implemented mechanisms.

What is the difference between MRDN and LOCK ?

LOCK is an Erc-20 token just like MRDN. The dapps within the Meridian ecosystem will only be accessible to users holding LOCK. MRDN was meant as a temporary token and will have no use once LOCK goes live. The reason we upgraded from MRDN to LOCK was due to the fact that the MRDN smart contract lacked the functionality required for our future DeFi dapps. You can swap your MRDN for LOCK through the swap portal which is live on our website. You can also purchase LOCK from exchanges such as Uniswap and others to come.

Will there be other exchanges ?

Yes, we’re currently considering new exchange listings, and will be informing the community when we decide on one. We are aware that Hotbit listed MRDN. It was something they did without consulting us, we played no part in this.

What is the value of LOCK ?

LOCK's value will derive from the many financial dapps we plan on creating in the future, of which the DAO balancer pool is the first one ( the Vault is actually the first one but that one is not a permanent dapp ). LOCK will be central to these decentralized applications and its use will increase with each new one
